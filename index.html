FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is JalanJalanMY live today?
The app is in active development. This site is a preview for partners and early-access travelers.
Which platforms are integrated?
Agoda and Klook for hotels and tours, plus Google Maps and Google Calendar for routing and scheduling.
How does the AI build an itinerary?
It uses your budget, dates and interests to plan routes, compare bookings and generate a day-by-day schedule, asking follow-ups when something's missing.
Do bookings happen inside the app?
JalanJalanMY confirms availability and routes you to partner booking flows with deep links and tracking.
