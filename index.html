Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about the app, booking flow, and data sources.

Is JalanJalanMY live today?
The app is in active development. This site is a preview for partners and early access.
Which platforms are integrated?
We are integrating Agoda and Klook for hotels and tours, plus Google Maps and Calendar for routing and scheduling.
How does the AI build an itinerary?
It uses your budget, dates, and interests to plan routes, compare bookings, and generate a day-by-day schedule.
Will bookings happen inside the app?
JalanJalanMY will confirm availability and route you to partner booking flows with deep links and tracking.
